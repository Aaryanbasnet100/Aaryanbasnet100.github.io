01 — ABOUT

Conceptual first, then hands-on.

My approach to engineering starts with "why" before "how". Whether it's backpropagation, memory management in operating systems, or Maxwell's equations, I want the underlying model before I write a line of code.

Before university I worked as a data analyst intern at 360VUZ in Dubai — collecting, cleaning and analysing datasets in Python, and turning results into reports and dashboards. That experience taught me the hard part is rarely the model; it's the messy data and the humans around it.

At AAU I've combined coursework in mathematics, physics, algorithms and operating systems with applied projects: a content-based video retrieval system built for the Video Browser Showdown competition, a real-time emotion detection pipeline, and ShiftFlow, a multi-tenant scheduling app.
